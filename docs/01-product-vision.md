# 01 — Product Vision

## Vision Statement

To become the most trusted hyperlocal marketplace where every household can find a verified, skilled worker within minutes, and every skilled worker can build a sustainable livelihood.

## Mission

Empower local service economies by giving workers digital identity, fair visibility, and tools to grow — while giving customers confidence, speed, and quality.

## North Star Metric

**Completed Jobs per Active Worker per Week.**

A worker successfully completing jobs indicates platform health: customers are hiring, workers are earning, and quality is being delivered.

## Supporting Metrics

| Metric                            | Why It Matters     |
| --------------------------------- | ------------------ |
| Daily Active Users (DAU)          | Engagement depth   |
| Job Post → First Application Time | Discovery speed    |
| Job Post → Assignment Time        | Fulfillment speed  |
| Average Worker Rating             | Quality signal     |
| Customer Repeat Hire Rate         | Trust signal       |
| Worker Churn (30-day)             | Worker-side health |
| Verified Worker %                 | Trust foundation   |

## Product Principles

1. **Trust first** — every worker is verifiable; every job is traceable.
2. **Speed** — post a job in under 60 seconds; get applications in minutes.
3. **Fairness** — workers gain visibility through skill, ratings, and proximity — not paid placement (initially).
4. **Clarity** — every state in the job lifecycle is explicit and observable.
5. **Locality** — proximity matters; hyperlocal relevance over global ranking.
6. **Reversibility** — easy to fix mistakes (rescind application, cancel job, dispute).

## User Experience Pillars

- **Customer**: simple posting → fast discovery → safe hiring → easy review.
- **Worker**: simple profile → steady leads → clear job details → fast payout (future).
- **Admin**: signal-first dashboard → fast moderation → actionable analytics.

## 3-Year Vision

- Year 1: MVP in 1 city, single category groups.
- Year 2: Multi-city, multi-category, realtime chat, maps.
- Year 3: Mobile apps, payments, subscriptions, contractor teams, marketplace flywheel.

## Anti-Goals (What We Will Not Do Initially)

- Become a general-purpose freelance marketplace (Upwork-style).
- Compete with on-demand delivery platforms.
- Replace traditional agency/contractor hierarchies.
