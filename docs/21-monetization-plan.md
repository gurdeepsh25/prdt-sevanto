# 21 — Monetization Plan

## Guiding Principle

**Do not monetize before liquidity.** Focus on trust, repeat usage, and worker supply density in MVP.

## Streams

### 1. Platform Commission (Phase 17)

- % of job value at completion.
- Range: 8–15%.
- Charged to customer (default) or worker (configurable).
- Invoiced automatically; refunds on disputes.

### 2. Worker Subscriptions (Phase 17)

| Tier | Price | Features                                                   |
| ---- | ----- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| Free | $0    | Standard profile, apply to jobs                            |
| Pro  | $X/mo | Verified Pro badge, priority placement, advanced analytics |
| Team | $Y/mo | Team management, bulk tools, invoicing (future)            |

### 3. Featured Listings (Phase 17)

- Workers pay for top placement in category results.
- Time-bound (7/30 days).
- Transparent "Sponsored" label.

### 4. Customer Convenience Fee (Phase 17)

- Optional add-on for dispute resolution + priority support.

### 5. Promotions / Banners (Post-Launch)

- Sponsored category banners.
- Local partner placements (e.g., paint brands for home repair category).

## Pricing Experiments

- A/B test commission % and subscription pricing by city.
- Track elasticity vs. conversion.

## Financial Plumbing

- Stripe (global) / Razorpay (India) integration.
- Stored payment methods (tokenized).
- Webhooks for payment status.
- Invoices + receipts (email + PDF).

## Accounting

- Separate ledger entries per transaction.
- Reconciliation reports for finance.
- Tax handling (future: per region).

## Revenue Dashboards (Admin)

- MRR by tier.
- Commission collected (week/month).
- Featured listing revenue.
- Churn %.

## Ethical Considerations

- No dark patterns in subscription management.
- One-click cancel.
- Refund policy transparent.
